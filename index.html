Privacy Policy

Shree Kalyan Gold & Silver respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us while using our mobile application.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use the app:

  • Mobile number (for investment-related access only)
  • Name and address (optional, provided by user)
  • Basic device information for app functionality
  • Investment-related records entered by the user or updated by the store

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as passwords, banking credentials, or card details.

2. Purpose of Information Usage

The collected information is used only for:

  • Managing investment-related features within the app
  • Displaying investment history to the user
  • Store communication and follow-up
  • Improving app experience and services

The app is not used for online payments or money transfers.

3. Investment & Payment Information

  • All payments related to investment schemes are handled offline directly at the store.
  • The app only records investment details for reference and tracking purposes.
  • Cash withdrawal or digital fund transfer through the app is not supported.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share user data with third parties.

User information is shared only:

  • With the store administration for scheme management
  • When required by law or legal authorities

5. WhatsApp & External Links

The app may redirect users to:

  • WhatsApp
  • Phone dialer
  • Email
  • Google Maps
  • Social media platforms

Please note that once redirected, the privacy policies of those external services apply.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user data using standard security practices. However, no digital platform can guarantee complete security.

Users are advised to:

  • Use their own mobile number
  • Keep their PIN confidential

7. User Responsibility

  • Users are responsible for providing correct personal information.
  • The app does not verify ownership of the entered mobile number through OTP or email.
  • The store will not be responsible for issues arising from incorrect or shared mobile numbers.
